About Me



Hello there! I'm Aria, a current student in the GIMM (Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technology) major at Boise State University. My focus in this major has been programming and audio, but I have also gained experience in 3D modeling, animation, and concepting. I would characterize myself as a generalist who can adapt their skills to any situation. I am a quick learner, and I am always looking to improve or hone my skills. As a hobby, I enjoy drawing, playing the piano, and making funny video edits of playing games with my friends, or things that I’ve experienced in my programming. Feel free to explore my portfolio of projects or contact me with any questions!
